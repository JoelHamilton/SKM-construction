OUR COMPANY



Our Companies


SKM Construction & Industry SL Ltd. leads a diverse group of companies dedicated to delivering excellence across various sectors. From cutting-edge construction and infrastructure projects to innovative food production at SKM Maggie Factory and reliable fuel solutions with SKM Petroleum, our companies work together to meet the needs of clients and communities with quality, integrity, and sustainability at the forefront.

SKM Construction & Industry SL Ltd.

Leading construction services, providing innovative solutions for both residential and commercial projects. SKM Construction & Industry SL Ltd. was founded in 2009 and has become a trusted name in the construction industry in Sierra Leone. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, SKM has completed a variety of projects, ranging from civil engineering to road and bridge construction.

SKM Maggie Factory

In a bid to diversify it's investment portfolio and operations, SKM GROUP established and invested in food seasoning industry called SKM Maggie Factory SL Ltd in Sumbuya, Koya Chiefdom, Port Loko District, Republic of Sierra Leone in 2021. The factory produces sweet Salon Cubes Seasoning, Sweet Salon Sachet Seasoning, Sweet salon Sachet White MSG etc. The company can also do OEM production for other brand to serve the country.

SKM Materials

SKM Materials is a leading provider of high-quality construction materials, specializing in sourcing and supplying products that meet the diverse needs of the construction industry. From cement, aggregates, and steel to other essential building materials, SKM Materials ensures that clients receive top-notch products for their projects. With a commitment to reliability and quality, SKM Materials plays a critical role in supporting infrastructure development across Sierra Leone, contributing to the successful completion of residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

SKM Petroleum

SKM Petroleum SL Ltd was incorporated in 2011 dealing principally in the importation, marketing and distribution of petroleum products, motor vehicle lubricants and services. The Company’s objective is to engage efficiently and responsibly and to maintain a long-term position in The Sierra Leone. Presently the company operate ten (10) Fuel stations located in various parts of the country and covers most of big cities accross Sierra Leone with a staff complement of One hundred and seventy six (176) employees.
