ABOUT US

Our Story

SKM Construction & Industry SL Ltd. was founded in 2009 and has become a trusted name in the construction industry in Sierra Leone. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, SKM has completed a variety of projects, ranging from civil engineering to road and bridge construction.


In the years that SKM has been in existence it has earned the respect of Sierra Leoneans at home and abroad, for reliability, superior quality and high integrity, and has fast become one of the indigenous construction companies in Sierra Leone.

The company now boasts an impressive technical team with full on-site professional support services to provide for all our client needs. puts paramount credibility, reliability and the quality of its services through fulfilling its commitments and contractual obligations even under very tight implementation schedules.

The Company has demonstrated experiences through executing successfully projects concerned with the construction of Roads, Barracks, Palm oil processing plants, Fuel Terminals, Fuel Stations and other private commercial and residential projects.

The Company provide all type of building materials like granite stone form SKM quarry at Mile 38 and finishing materials like all kind of zinc from SKM Zinc factory at Calaba Town, Windows and doors, Tiles and PVC Ceiling and Kitchen Cabinet. The Company also provide rental service for all kind of equipment and Machinery like excavator, bulldozer, front loader, crane, concrete mixer truck, and tippers for haulage works.



Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations while ensuring sustainability and safety in all our operations.
