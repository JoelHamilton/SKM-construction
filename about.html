ABOUT US

Our Story

SKM Construction & Industry SL Ltd. was founded in 2009 and has become a trusted name in the construction industry in Sierra Leone. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, SKM has completed a variety of projects, ranging from civil engineering to road and bridge construction.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations while ensuring sustainability and safety in all our operations.

Key Achievements

  • Successfully completed the construction of a 500-unit modern barracks complex for the Ministry of Defence.
  • Rehabilitation of Mabonto 7.4 Km feeder road.
  • Education Admin Buildings for Prince of Wales Secondary School.
